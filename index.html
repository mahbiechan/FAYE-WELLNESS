MASSAGE SERVICES

HOT STONE MASSAGE

Hot stone massage uses warm, smooth stones placed on key points of the body to release deep muscle tension. The heat improves blood flow, reduces stress, and promotes relaxation. Ideal for soothing soreness and melting away fatigue, it delivers deep comfort and tranquility.

REFLEXOLOGY MASSAGE

Reflexology focuses on pressure points in the feet, hands, or ears that correspond to different organs in the body. It encourages natural healing, improves circulation, and reduces tension. This deeply relaxing therapy restores balance and relieves stress.

SHIATSU MASSAGE

Shiatsu massage is a Japanese technique using finger and palm pressure to stimulate energy flow and balance the body. It relieves tension, improves circulation, and promotes emotional well-being. Performed without oils, Shiatsu enhances relaxation, flexibility, and overall body harmony.

SPORT MASSAGE

Sports massage is designed for athletes and active individuals. It targets specific muscle groups to improve flexibility, reduce soreness, and prevent injuries. Using deep pressure and stretching techniques, it enhances performance, speeds recovery, and keeps the body in peak condition.

SWEDISH MASSAGE

Swedish massage is a classic relaxation technique using smooth, gliding strokes and gentle kneading. It improves circulation, reduces muscle tension, and relieves stress. Perfect for first-time clients or those seeking relaxation, this massage leaves the body refreshed, calm, and fully restored from daily fatigue.

THAI MASSAGE

Thai massage combines acupressure, stretching, and yoga-like movements to restore balance and flexibility. Performed on a mat without oils, it increases energy flow, relieves stiffness, and promotes overall well-being. This ancient healing technique leaves you feeling recharged, flexible, and deeply relaxed.
